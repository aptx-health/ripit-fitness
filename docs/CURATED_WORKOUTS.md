# Curated Workouts - Iron Works Boulder

Pre-built workout content for the quick start flow. Designed for Iron Works Fitness (Boulder, CO) — a neighborhood gym with an older-skewing, health-focused population and a smaller contingent of younger lifters who want structured training.

See `GYM_EQUIPMENT.md` for equipment reference and curation notes.

## Design Principles

- **Proximity-aware**: exercises stay within the same room/zone to reduce wandering
- **Approachability first**: machines are the on-ramp; barbells are earned, not assumed
- **Weight-bearing loading**: especially for older adults and bone health, but benefits everyone
- **Cable work featured throughout**: versatile, joint-friendly, low intimidation, two stations across two rooms
- **No TRX**: too technique-dependent for this population without coaching
- **Smith machine Tier 3+ only**: requires more instruction for safe use
- **Intensity via RIR/RPE**: most programs use RIR (Reps in Reserve). Barbell-heavy programs (5x5, Powerbuilder) use RPE for tighter intensity control.

## 5-Week Periodization

All gym programs follow a 5-week build-up / peak / deload structure:

| Week | Intent | Intensity |
|------|--------|-----------|
| 1 | **Intro** — acclimate, learn equipment | Most conservative |
| 2 | **Building** — increasing intensity slightly | Moderate |
| 3 | **Steady** — holding intensity, building strength | Moderate |
| 4 | **Peak** — most intense sets | Highest |
| 5 | **Deload** — recover after peak | Back off |

### Taxing Exercise RIR Offset

Certain exercises always run +1 RIR (lower intensity) relative to other exercises in the same week:

- **All tiers**: Leg Press, Seated Leg Curl (very taxing / significant DOMS)
- **Tier 1-2**: Back Extension, Romanian Deadlift (hazardous or demanding for newer lifters)
- **Tier 3+**: Barbell Squat, Romanian Deadlift (heavy compounds)

## Prescription Framework

| Tier | Sets/Exercise | Compound Reps | Machine/Isolation Reps | Intensity |
|------|---------------|---------------|------------------------|-----------|
| 1 (Beginner) | 2 | n/a | 10-12 | RIR 3-5 |
| 2 (Beginner-Int) | 2-3 | 8-10 | 10-12 | RIR 2-4 |
| 3 (Intermediate) | 3 | 6-8 | 10-12 | RIR 2-4 |
| 4 (Barbell/Heavy) | 3-5 | 5 | 8-10 | RPE 6-8.5 |
| Home | 2-4 | 10-20 | n/a | RIR 2-3 |

Weight is left blank — user fills in based on their ability.

## Programs

### 1. Machine Starter

**Level**: Beginner | **Frequency**: 2-3x/week | **Weeks**: 5 | **Equipment**: Machines only

Day-one program. All machines, fixed movement paths, low cognitive load. Alternating A/B full body workouts. Starts with lower volume and intensity, gradually ramps up.

| Day | Workout | Exercises |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Machine Full Body A | Chest Press, Shoulder Press, Lat Pulldown, Leg Press, Leg Extensions, Seated Leg Curl, Back Extension |
| 2 | Machine Full Body B | Chest Supported Row, Dip Machine, Leg Press, Thigh Adductor, Thigh Abductor, Ab Crunch Machine, Back Extension |

---

### 2. Confidence Builder

**Level**: Beginner | **Frequency**: 3-4x/week | **Weeks**: 5 | **Equipment**: Machines + dumbbells + cables

Introduces dumbbells and cables alongside machines. Three-day rotation including Foundation Strength day for bone density.

| Day | Workout | Exercises |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Upper Body - Machines & Cables | Chest Press, Chest Supported Row, Shoulder Press, Lat Pulldown, Cable Curl, Triceps Pushdown |
| 2 | Lower Body - Machines & Light DBs | Leg Press, Leg Extensions, Seated Leg Curl, Goblet Squat, Romanian Deadlift, Thigh Adductor |
| 3 | Foundation Strength | Leg Press, Chest Press, Lat Pulldown, Farmer's Walk, DB Step Ups, Back Extension |

---

### 3. Full Body Strength

**Level**: Intermediate | **Frequency**: 3x/week | **Weeks**: 5 | **Equipment**: Barbell + machines

Compound-heavy A/B full body. Good step up from Confidence Builder for those who prefer fewer gym days.

| Day | Workout | Exercises |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Full Body Strength A | DB Bench Press, Barbell Squat, Chest Supported Row, DB Shoulder Press, Seated Leg Curl, Face Pull |
| 2 | Full Body Strength B | Romanian Deadlift, Lat Pulldown, Standing DB Press, Leg Press, Cable Lateral Raise, Triceps Pushdown |

### 4. Push / Pull / Legs

**Level**: Intermediate | **Frequency**: 3-6x/week | **Weeks**: 5 | **Equipment**: Barbell + dumbbells + cables + machines

Classic PPL split. Each day focuses on one movement pattern.

| Day | Workout | Exercises |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Push | DB Bench Press, Incline DB Press, Cable Crossover, Side Lateral Raise, Triceps Pushdown, Dip Machine |
| 2 | Pull | Lat Pulldown, Chest Supported Row, DB Row, Face Pull, Cable Curl, Machine Preacher Curls |
| 3 | Legs & Core | Barbell Squat, Leg Press, Romanian Deadlift, Leg Extensions, Seated Leg Curl, Cable Wood Chop |

### 5. Nothing But Cables*

**Level**: Beginner | **Frequency**: 3x/week | **Weeks**: 5 | **Equipment**: Cables + dumbbells + machines

Cable-focused program with some dumbbell and machine work on lower body days. Joint-friendly, great pump. *We tried making it literally nothing but cables, but lower body needed some help.

| Day | Workout | Exercises |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Upper | Cable Chest Press, Lat Pull-Around, Cable Lateral Raise, Face Pull, Cable Curl, Triceps Pushdown |
| 2 | Lower & Core | DB Bulgarian Split Squat, Goblet Squat, Pull Through, Cable Wood Chop, Cable Crunch, Standing Calf Raise |
| 3 | Full Body | DB Lunges, Leg Extensions, Cable Chest Press, Seated Cable Rows, Face Pull, Cable Curl, Triceps Pushdown, Pull Through |

### 6. Barbell Basics 5x5

**Level**: Intermediate | **Frequency**: 3x/week | **Weeks**: 5 | **Equipment**: Barbell + cables

Starting Strength-adjacent. Three big compound lifts at 5x5 plus cable accessories. Trap bar deadlift over conventional for safety. Uses RPE for intensity — start at ~75% of 1RM.

| Day | Workout | Exercises |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Barbell Basics A | Barbell Squat 5x5, Bench Press 5x5, Bent Over Row 5x5, Cable Curl, Face Pull |
| 2 | Barbell Basics B | Trap Bar Deadlift 5x5, Standing DB Press 5x5, Lat Pulldown 5x5, Triceps Pushdown, Cable Lateral Raise |

## Exercise Name Reference

Exercise names use canonical names from the free exercise DB (github.com/yuhonas/free-exercise-db) where possible. Custom definitions not in that DB:

- **Chest Supported Row** — Iron Works has a dedicated machine
- **Chest Press** — free DB uses "Leverage Chest Press"
- **Shoulder Press** — free DB uses "Machine Shoulder (Military) Press"
- **Lat Pulldown** — free DB uses "Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown"
- **Back Extension** — free DB uses "Hyperextensions (Back Extensions)"
- **Triceps Pushdown** — free DB uses "Triceps Pushdown - Rope Attachment"

These definitions are created by `prisma/seeds/curated/exercise-defs.ts` and will be reconciled when the free exercise DB is imported.

## Technical Implementation

### Data Flow

1. Programs were authored using the app's program editor (seeded as user Programs, then edited in-app)
2. Finalized programs were exported as JSON snapshots (`prisma/seeds/curated/snapshots/`)
3. `seed-community-programs.ts` reads snapshots and inserts as CommunityProgram rows with `curated: true`
4. Quick start wizard queries `CommunityProgram WHERE curated = true` filtered by level/equipment/goals

### Key Files

- `prisma/seeds/curated/snapshots/*.json` — program data (source of truth)
- `prisma/seeds/curated/seed-community-programs.ts` — idempotent seeder
- `prisma/seeds/curated/exercise-defs.ts` — exercise definitions needed by these programs
- `prisma/seeds/curated/helpers.ts` — shared types and utilities
- `prisma/seeds/curated/programs/*.ts` — week-1 specs (used for initial authoring, snapshots are final)
